About Let'SwapGames

Your platform to swap games with other players for free.

How does it work?

You just say which games you want and which games you have available for swapping and we automatically give you the best matches and the nearest user to conclude the swapping.

is it safe?

We do our best to build a trustworthy gamers community. But always check the rank and social network of the players you swap games with. If you have any problem, give a bad review on the user and report the issue to us.

Is it free?

Absolutely! And no such thing as paid or premium plans here.

More information

Why can I only sign-in with a social account?

Our community is based on trust. We need evidence of who you are, so other players will be more confident to swap games with you.

In which countries is it available?

We are from Ireland, but it's a global community. Don't worry, you'll swap games with other players from the same country as you.

Who are we, anyway?

We are gamers from Ireland who were tired of trying to sell and swap games in ad sites and being ripped of by our local game store...
