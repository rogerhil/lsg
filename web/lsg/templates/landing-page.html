About Let'SwapGames

Your platform to swap games with other players for free.

How does it work?

You just say which games you want and which games you have available for swapping. Then we automatically give you the best matches and the nearest user to swap with.

Is it safe?

We do our best to build a trustworthy gamers community, and you can always check the rank and social network of the players you swap games with. If you have any problem, give a bad review on the user and report the issue.

Is it free?

Absolutely! And no such thing as paid or premium plans here.

FAQ

Why can I only sign up with a social media account?

Our community is based on trust. We need evidence of who you are, so other players will be more confident to swap games with you.

In which countries is it available?

At the moment only in Ireland, United Kingdom and Isle of Man. But pretty soon we'll be all over the world. So sign up to be the first to know when it's available in your country.

Who are you?

We are gamers from Ireland who were tired of trying to sell and swap games in ad sites, forums and groups, and having a hard time finding users to swap games with. So we decided to scratch our own itch, and made a whole platform to make swapping games easy.
